About EXPEDIFY

EXPEDIFY is your ultimate travel companion, designed to help you plan, track, and enhance your travel experiences. With an interactive world map, you can mark your traveled areas, set desired destinations, and earn badges as you explore new places. Whether you're a frequent traveler or an occasional adventurer, EXPEDIFY offers you a personalized and engaging way to manage your journeys.
